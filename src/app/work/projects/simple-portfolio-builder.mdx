## Overview

The Gold Invest Platform is a comprehensive digital gold investment platform developed for a US client using React and Node.js with Supabase as the backend. The platform empowers users to invest in gold easily and securely, providing real-time price tracking, fractional gold purchases, portfolio management, and educational resources. Users can buy and sell gold instantly, monitor their holdings, and access insights to make informed investment decisions.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Gold Prices**: Fetches live gold prices from trusted market APIs, allowing users to make informed investment decisions instantly.
- **Buy & Sell Gold**: Users can purchase gold in grams or fractional units and liquidate their holdings at real-time rates.
- **Portfolio Management**: Tracks investments, displays current value, historical trends, and performance analytics on a user-friendly dashboard.
- **Gold Savings Plans**: Supports recurring investments and customizable schedules for consistent portfolio growth.
- **Secure Wallet & Transactions**: Digital wallet for holding funds and gold; transactions secured with JWT authentication, HTTPS, and Supabase backend.
- **Price Alerts & Notifications**: Users receive notifications when gold reaches target prices or significant market changes occur.
- **Educational Insights**: Provides market analysis, trends, and guides to educate users about gold investment.
- **Referral Program**: Encourages users to invite friends for rewards and bonuses.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React.js with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS for responsive and interactive design
- **Backend**: Node.js and Express.js for secure, high-performance APIs
- **Database & Authentication**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage) for secure data storage and management
- **External APIs**: Gold price feeds from trusted international and local markets
- **Deployment & DevOps**: Vercel for frontend, Supabase hosting for backend, GitHub Actions for CI/CD
- **Security**: JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and secure Supabase storage

## Challenges and Learnings

Integrating live gold market data with instant buy/sell functionality required robust backend architecture and error handling. Ensuring secure digital wallet transactions and compliance with US financial regulations was another challenge, which we addressed using Supabase authentication and encrypted storage. The project strengthened skills in **React frontend development, Node.js backend architecture, secure financial transactions, and Supabase integration**, while providing hands-on experience in building a real-world fintech platform for an international client.

## Outcome

The Gold Invest Platform successfully delivers a seamless digital gold investment experience. Users can securely invest in gold, monitor their portfolio in real-time, and make informed investment decisions. Key achievements include:

- Real-time gold price tracking and instant buy/sell functionality
- Secure digital wallet and transactions using Supabase
- Portfolio tracking with performance analytics
- Recurring investment plans for consistent growth
- Mobile-responsive and cross-platform usability

The platform is production-ready and demonstrates the potential of digital solutions in democratizing gold investment. Future enhancements may include **AI-driven investment advice, multi-currency support, enhanced social features, and mobile applications**.
